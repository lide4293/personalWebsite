Hobbies Page

I love to crochet! I have made a few tops, a pillow case, hats, flowers, bookmarks, and am currently working on some cute striped shorts! It is a great way to relax and make something creative.

Journaling

I find journaling to be a creative way to put my thoughts down. It also serves as a great memento to look back on! I have bullet journals, reading journals, and highlight journals to keep track of everything!
